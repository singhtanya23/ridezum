What we do

From designing optimized transportation solutions to fulfilling daily transportation needs, Zūm is a one-stop partner for a school district of any size.
Transportation for all student needs

Whether it’s home-to-school transportation, off-campus events, or delivery of meals and educational materials — we’re committed to meeting the dynamic daily needs of your unique student population. We’ll handle routes for public, private, charter schools, and non-public schools, McKinney-Vento, and out-of-district placements. You’ll enjoy efficiency and cost savings using a fleet of right-sized vehicles.

End-to-end technology for you and your entire team

Simplify route management, school and student onboarding and get 360 degree visibility through our proprietary technology. Improve collaboration and communication between your entire team, vendors, drivers and parents on a single platform. You get flexibility, control, transparency, and efficiency.

Superior customer success teams to help you every step of the way

With Zūm, your teams will receive bilingual support over email, text and the phone. From proactive alerts of potential issues to complete case management, we’re here to meet your daily transportation needs. Along with incident reporting and documentation, you’ll gain valuable insights via regular reports.

Our services are built to maximize the benefits to schools

We plan and optimize your routes and schedules using our proprietary technology and take care of daily transport operations. Streamline your transportation capacity with our network of any size vehicles. It’s better for your bottom line and the environment.
